TestCommSend replaces both TestBasic and TestParallelSend.

Comm.Send is both asynchronous and lossy, so the current tests which
assumed a 100% success rate were improperly designed.

Removed TestProdConstructor as it was an almost exact duplicate of TestBasic.

practice, the racing issues are both very unlikely, and very
recoverable if the users understand that the channels are lossy and
have no guarantees of throughput.

Refactor TestAccept with understanding of the same behavior as TestCommSend.
