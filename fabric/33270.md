Add the ability to pause/resume channels when a peer is stopped.
When a channel is paused on a peer, the peer will not start
resources for the channel and will not receive new blocks
or process requests for the channel. When a channel is resumed,
the peer will receive blocks and process channel requests.

Completely agree that from a user perspective it makes much more sense as an online update, it's more a question of timing and staging.

For the short term, we want to get the ledger infrastructure in place and have at least SOME way to pause/resume.
That SOME way with least investment is via CLI command for now, and will likely in the short term be used in combination with peer reset/rollback which is anyways also a CLI command.

At this time, the AdminServer grpc APIs have been removed, we don't want to extend system chaincodes for peer administration as those are expected to be removed next, and we don't yet have a strategic replacement in place.
Once the strategic replacement for peer administration is in place I certainly expect pause/resume and other peer administration functions such as channel join and a new channel unjoin/delete would get added as online functions.
